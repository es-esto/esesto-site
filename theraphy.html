THERAPHY

/ app

Project Role:
Product Designer - UX/UI

Goal:
To design an app that promotes self-discovery and personal growth by making philosophical insights accessible and relatable. The focus was on creating a clear, elegant, and user-friendly platform that empowers users to reflect on themselves and the world.

Process:

  • Conducted user interviews and surveys to explore how individuals engage with philosophical concepts and personal development tools.
  • Created user personas and journey maps to understand diverse user motivations and learning styles.
  • Performed usability testing to ensure that the app's navigation and content delivery were seamless and intuitive.
  • Designed a refined and minimalist visual identity to complement the clarity and depth of the app's content.

Outcome:
Theraphy successfully blends philosophy and personal growth into a meaningful user experience. The final design provides users with clear, accessible content while fostering a sense of connection and reflection. The app balances elegance and simplicity, offering a platform for self-exploration and understanding.
